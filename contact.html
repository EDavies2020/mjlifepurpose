1. All payments are to be received a minimum of a week before the allotted session(s) scheduled unless the service agreed is booked within a week of operation. In this instance, payments must be made immediately to avoid any delay or disappointment.

2. A minimum of 48 hours notice is required at all times for cancellations or change of appointment.

3. Cancellations or alterations to bookings within 48hrs of a session booking will incur a 30 pound cancellation and admin fee.

4. Bookings cancelled on the same day will incur a forfeit of the agreed session

5. All bookings will take place online via Facetime or Skype as agreed by both parties unless an arrangement has been made prior to the appointment to work within the studio or at your home.

6. Please note a minimum of 10 pound travel surcharge will be added to the session if the session takes place at your home.
